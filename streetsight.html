Street Sight

An award-winning integrated project developed during Term 3 of the Web and Mobile Application Design program at Langara College. Designed to streamline billboard tracking, performance analysis, and client relationship management for advertising agencies. The goal of StreetSight is to streamline billboard management for advertising agencies by providing a centralized platform to monitor, organize, and evaluate billboard assets and campaign performance. The CRM is designed to improve operational efficiency, enhance client communication, and support data-driven decision-making through real-time insights and interactive map-based visualization tools.
