Pawll

Year 2024
Academic Project UI UX Design
Categories Service App
Project Goal

The goal of Pawll is to create a user-friendly mobile application that connects pet owners with trusted caregivers, groomers, and veterinary services. The app aims to simplify pet care management by offering convenient booking, real-time updates, and personalized service options, ensuring pets receive the highest level of care while giving owners peace of mind.
