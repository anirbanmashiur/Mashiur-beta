Website Redesign

The goal of this redesign was to modernize the Stallions Cricket website by creating a clean, user-friendly interface that reflects the brand’s professionalism and energy. The focus was on improving navigation, enhancing visual impact, and making it easier for users to explore coaching programs, book sessions, and connect with the team—ultimately creating a seamless experience for players, parents, and visitors.

Home Page

Before: The homepage lacked a clear visual hierarchy, leaving visitors unsure where to focus. Important actions like program sign-ups and bookings were buried, and the overall layout felt cluttered.
After: The redesign introduces a modern, spacious layout with a strong hero section, clear calls-to-action, and defined content sections. Updated typography and high-quality imagery guide the user journey, making it easier for visitors to understand offerings and take action.

Coaching Programs Page

Before: The coaching programs page was text-heavy, making it difficult for users to scan and compare options. The layout lacked visual engagement and structure.
After: The new design uses card-based layouts, visuals, and icons to break down each program into an easy-to-read format. Key details like session times, pricing, and benefits are highlighted, improving both readability and conversion potential.

Branding

Incorporated the company’s existing logo, typography, and color palette to ensure the website aligns with brand guidelines and delivers a visually cohesive experience.

Components

I focused on creating modular and reusable components to streamline development and maintain visual consistency throughout the site. This approach not only improved usability and accessibility but also allowed for easier future updates and scalability.

Site Map

I developed a streamlined sitemap to simplify the website’s structure and improve user navigation. The new sitemap organizes content hierarchically with clear categories and pathways, making it easier for users to find information quickly. This improved structure lays the foundation for a more intuitive browsing experience and supports the overall goals of the redesign by aligning the site’s architecture with user needs.
